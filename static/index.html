Connecting…
  1. Enter text to synthesize — up to 5 000 characters.
  2. Choose a speaker and language. The style instruction field lets you shape tone and emotion separately from the spoken text.
  3. Adjust Post-Processing — upsample, normalize, soft clip, and pseudo-stereo are all on by default.
  4. Pick WAV (lossless) or MP3 as the output format.
  5. Click Generate and wait — first generation loads the model; subsequent ones are fast.
  6. Play the result inline or use Download to save the file.
